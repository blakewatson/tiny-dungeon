Rules

When you enter a room (other than the Shop), there’s a chance you’ll find a Monster (50%), a trap (~17%), an Item (~17%), or an empty room (~17%).

Once you clear all rooms on a level, proceed to the Den to ascend to the next level.

Races

Your race is chosen randomly when you roll a character. Depending on your race, you get an attack bonus against certain Monsters.

  • Halfling (+1 vs. Rats and Dragons)
  • Dwarf (+1 vs. Goblins and Trolls)
  • Elf (+1 vs. Orcs and Skeleton mages)

Each race has a maximum of 3 hit points.

Monsters

♥ = 1 hit point

  • Rat ♥
  • Goblin ♥♥
  • Orc ♥♥
  • Skeleton mage ♥♥♥
  • Troll ♥♥♥
  • Dragon ♥♥♥♥♥ (only found on levels above 1)

Monsters have a 50% chance of dropping an Item.

Items

  • 1 gold coin
  • 2 gold coins
  • Sword (+1 attack)
  • Armor (+1 defense)
  • Healing potion ♥ (+1 hit point when used)
  • Amulet (+1 attack and +1 against traps)
  • Amulet of Yendor! ★ (chance of finding in a room is ~1.4%)

How combat works

For every +1 attack, you are rolling a d6 and then taking the highest roll as your attack.

For every +1 defense, the monster is rolling a d6 and then taking the lowest roll as its attack.

An attack roll is successful on a 4, 5, or 6.

Traps

A trap is disarmed successfully on a roll of 4, 5, or 6. Otherwise, the trap is triggered and you lose 1 hit point.

Amulets provide +1 against traps. This means for every amulet you have you are rolling a d6 and then taking the highest roll.

Winning

You win immediately upon discovering the Amulet of Yendor! ★
